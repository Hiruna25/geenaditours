Detailed Itinerary

Day 01: Colombo / Bentota

After arrival you will meet our representative at the arrival lounge and will be welcomed. Then you will be transferred to Bentota.

Spend rest of your day leisure at the beach.

Overnight stay in Bentota

Day 02: Bentota / Galle

After breakfast leave the hotel and travel to Galle.

Optional Activities:

  • Madhu River Boat Ride
  • Turtle Hatchery Visit
  • Water sports in Bentota river

Bentota, situated on the Northern limit of the Southern province at the confluence of the sea at the Bentota river, 62 km from Colombo. Its location in the sun drenched southern coast at the mouth of Bentota River and its picturesque, sandy, palm-fringed beaches which border the warm, sparkling waters of the Indian Ocean made it a natural choice for development as Sri Lanka's first planned tourist resort.

Overnight stay in Bentota

Day 03: Colombo City Tour

After breakfast check out and proceed to Colombo. Enjoy your city tour in Colombo.

Sightseeing Highlights:

  • Galle Face Green - Ocean-side urban park
  • Old Parliament Building - Historic colonial architecture
  • Gangaramaya Temple - Important Buddhist temple
  • Independence Square - National monument
  • Viharamahadevi Park - Largest park in Colombo
  • Shopping in Colombo - Visit local markets and malls

Overnight in Colombo

Day 04: Departure

After breakfast check-out from the hotel.

Then proceed to Airport for your departure flight.

You must be present at the airport on or before 3 hours prior to the flight time
