Detailed Itinerary

Day 01: Airport / Kandy

After arrival you will meet our representative at the arrival lounge and will be welcomed. Then you will be transferred to Kandy.

Sightseeing in Kandy:

  • Sacred Tooth Relic Temple: The Sri Dalada Maligawa or The Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic is a temple in the city of Kandy in Sri Lanka. It was built within the royal palace complex which houses the tooth relic of the Buddha.
  • Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage: See elephants in their natural habitat.
  • Spice & Herbal Garden: Learn about Sri Lanka's famous spices.
  • Peradeniya Botanical Garden: Beautiful botanical gardens with exotic plants.
  • Kandy Lake & Upper Lake Drive: Scenic views of Kandy.
  • Cultural Dance Show: Traditional Kandyan dancing.

Overnight stay in Kandy

Day 02: Kandy / Nuwara Eliya

En-route visit tea plantations, tea factories, Ramboda waterfall and Sri Bhakta Hanuman temple.

Highlights:

  • Tea Plantations: Tea was first planted in Sri Lanka in 1824 at the Botanical Gardens at Peradeniya. Today more than 200,000 hectares in highlands are under tea cultivation.
  • Ramboda Waterfall: Beautiful waterfall on the way to Nuwara Eliya.
  • Hanuman Temple: Important Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Hanuman.

Overnight in Nuwara Eliya

Day 03: Nuwara Eliya / Bentota

After breakfast leave the hotel and travel to Bentota.

En-route Visits:

  • St. Clare's Falls
  • Devon Falls
  • Kitulgala White Water Rafting (Optional)
  • Madhu River (Optional)

Bentota Activities:

  • Turtle Hatchery (Optional)
  • Water sports in Bentota river (Optional)

Bentota is famed in Sri Lanka's global tourist markets as highly attractive holiday resort with wide and varied choice of distinctive hotels.

Overnight stay at Bentota

Day 04: Bentota

Spend the day at leisure enjoying the beautiful beach and resort facilities.

Overnight stay at Bentota

Day 05: Bentota / Airport

After breakfast check-out from the hotel.

Then proceed to Airport for your departure flight.

You must be present at the airport on or before 3 hours prior to the flight time

End of Tour
